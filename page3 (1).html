Peru

What is the capital of Peru ? Lima is the capital of Peru.
Where is Peru's location? Peru is located in South America.

Where to visit in Peru?

  1. Machu Picchu: Explore the iconic Inca citadel high in the Andes Mountains.
  2. Lima: Discover the historic city's colonial architecture, museums, and vibrant markets.
  3. Cusco: Visit the ancient Inca city, explore the Sacred Valley, and hike the Inca Trail.
  4. Lake Titicaca: Experience the unique culture and landscapes of the highest navigable lake in the world.
  5. Amazon Rainforest: Explore the lush rainforest, encounter diverse wildlife, and visit indigenous communities.

Why should you visit Peru?
You should visit Peru because of its diverse natural beauty and rich culture.
